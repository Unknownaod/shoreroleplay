Server Rules

All players are expected to operate within these guidelines at all times.

1 — Community Conduct

  • Respect all members and staff at all times.
  • No harassment, hate speech, bullying, or discrimination.
  • Staff instructions must be followed without argument.

2 — Roleplay Standards

  • Characters must behave realistically — no FailRP, trolling, or absurd behavior.
  • No metagaming, powergaming, or exploiting mechanics.
  • Story-building and progression are expected at all times.

3 — Crime & Combat

  • Random killing (RDM) and vehicle attacks (VDM) are strictly prohibited.
  • Major crimes must be supported by proper RP.
  • Value your life — act as if consequences matter.

4 — Vehicles & Pursuits

  • No unrealistic vehicles unless approved.
  • Pursuit tactics must be logical and grounded in RP.

5 — Discord Requirements

  • You must remain in the official Discord server at all times.
  • Impersonation of staff or departments results in removal.

6 — Enforcement

  • Violations will result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans.
  • Staff decisions are final.
